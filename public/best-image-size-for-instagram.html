2025 Guide to Instagram Photo Dimensions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best image size for Instagram posts in 2025?
For square posts, use 1080×1080 pixels. For portrait posts (which get more engagement), use 1080×1350 pixels (4:5 ratio). For landscape, use 1080×566 pixels. Always upload at 1080 pixels width minimum for best quality.
What size should Instagram Stories be?
Instagram Stories should be 1080×1920 pixels (9:16 aspect ratio). This is full-screen vertical format. Keeping important content in the center vertical third ensures it's visible even with UI elements.
Why do my Instagram photos look blurry after uploading?
Instagram compresses images that don't meet their specifications. To prevent this: upload at 1080 pixels width, use JPG format at 70-85% quality, and avoid uploading images that are too large (Instagram re-compresses them).
