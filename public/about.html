About MyGuyAI

Our Mission

MyGuyAI was founded with a simple mission: to provide fast, free, and secure online tools that respect your privacy. We believe that essential utilities like image compression, resizing, and file conversion should be accessible to everyone without compromising on security or usability.

What We Do

We offer a comprehensive suite of free online tools designed to help you manage your digital files efficiently:

  • Image Compression: Reduce image file sizes to exact targets (20KB, 50KB, 100KB, and more) while maintaining quality
  • Image Resizing: Resize images to specific dimensions for any purpose
  • Image Cropping: Crop images with various aspect ratios
  • Format Conversion: Convert between JPG, PNG, WebP, and other formats
  • PDF Tools: Compress, merge, and manage PDF documents
  • Passport Photos: Create properly sized photos for official documents

Privacy First Approach

Unlike many online tools, we take a fundamentally different approach to privacy:

  • 100% Client-Side Processing: All file processing happens directly in your web browser
  • No File Uploads: Your images and documents are never uploaded to our servers
  • Complete Privacy: We cannot see, access, or store any of your files
  • No Registration Required: Use all our tools instantly without creating an account

This approach means your sensitive documents, personal photos, and confidential files remain completely private on your device at all times.
