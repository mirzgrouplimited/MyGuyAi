Frequently Asked Questions

What is the photo size for SSC form?
SSC forms typically require photos between 20KB and 50KB in JPG format. The dimensions should be 3.5×4.5 cm or as specified in the notification. Signature images usually need to be 10KB-20KB.
How to compress photo to 20KB for SSC?
Upload your photo to our tool and select '20KB' as the target size. Our smart compression algorithm reduces file size while preserving facial clarity. Download the compressed image and upload directly to the SSC portal.
Why is my SSC photo getting rejected after compression?
Common reasons include: wrong dimensions (check pixel requirements), wrong format (must be JPG), over-compression causing blur, or content issues like wrong background color. Ensure your original photo meets SSC guidelines before compressing.
