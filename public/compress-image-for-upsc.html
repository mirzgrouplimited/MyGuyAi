Frequently Asked Questions

What is the photo size for UPSC Civil Services form?
UPSC Civil Services typically requires photos between 20KB and 300KB in JPG format. The dimensions vary by stage—check the official notification. Photos must have a white background with the candidate looking directly at the camera.
How to compress photo for UPSC without losing quality?
Use our smart compression tool that targets exact file sizes while preserving facial details. Start with a high-quality source photo against a white background. Our algorithm prioritizes facial area clarity during compression.
Can I use the same photo for UPSC Prelims and Mains?
Yes, as long as the photo meets the specifications for both stages. Requirements may differ slightly, so compress separate versions if needed. Always use a recent photo taken within the timeframe specified by UPSC.
