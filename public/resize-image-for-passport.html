Frequently Asked Questions

What size is a US passport photo in pixels?
A US passport photo must be exactly 2×2 inches, which equals 600×600 pixels at the required 300 DPI. For online applications through the State Department, the photo must also be under 240KB in JPG format.
How do I resize a photo for UK passport?
UK passport photos must be 35×45mm. For online applications, this translates to at least 600×750 pixels. Upload your photo to our tool, select 'UK Passport' preset, and it will resize to exact specifications while maintaining quality.
Can I resize my phone photo for passport application?
Yes, if your phone photo is high quality with a plain background. Our tool can resize it to exact passport dimensions. However, ensure the original photo meets content requirements: white background, neutral expression, and proper face positioning.
